Dentist Privacy Policy

At Udenz Hello, we understand the importance of privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you access or use the Udenz Hello website and its services specifically designed for dentists (collectively referred to as the "Platform").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information: When you create an account or interact with Udenz Hello as a dentist, we may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, professional qualifications, clinic information, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

1.2 Patient Data: As a dentist using Udenz Hello, you may upload patient data to the Platform. We treat patient data with the utmost confidentiality and security, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Providing Services: We may use your information to deliver the services and features of Udenz Hello specifically tailored for dentists, including appointment scheduling, patient management, and communication tools.

2.2 Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about the Platform's features, updates, and relevant dental industry information. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time.

2.3 Analytics and Improvements: We may use your information for analytical purposes, such as monitoring platform usage patterns, improving the Platform's functionality, and enhancing user experience.

3. Information Sharing

3.1 Patient Data Privacy: We understand the sensitive nature of patient data and will not share it with any third parties without your explicit consent, except as required by law or in accordance with your instructions.

3.2 Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating, maintaining, and improving Udenz Hello. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are prohibited from using your information for any other purpose.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

We are committed to complying with applicable data protection laws and regulations. As a dentist using Udenz Hello, it is your responsibility to ensure that your use of the Platform complies with all applicable privacy laws and regulations, including obtaining necessary consents from your patients.

6. Third-Party Links

Udenz Hello may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be effective as of the date stated at the beginning of the policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the use of your personal information, please contact us at support@udenz.ae.

By using Udenz Hello, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
